about study-well

study-well is an app designed to guide students through their education whether you're starting your journey from secondary school or applying to a university best suited to you. Through a number of helpful links tailored for your specific age group to socializing through a friendly forum, students can gain help whilst giving advice to other students who are also seeking help. With a page dedicated to detect mental health issues and improve students wellbeing, study-well wants to makes sure everyone is studying well whilst maintaining a healthier mindset.
